Enya - Memory of Trees

The Memory of Trees invites to reflect on the memory of trees, silent witnesses to human events and nature's evolution through the changing seasons. Trees are symbols of continuity, growth, and resilience. Although they are not directly mentioned in the song, we can imagine them as guardians of history and life itself. From an ecological perspective, it's impossible not to highlight how essential trees are for our planet. Without trees, life as we know it wouldn't exist, just as it wouldn't without water.
